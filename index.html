Short Bio

Qinde Chen is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in School of Software Engineering (软件工程) of Sun Yat-sen University (中山大学)，supervised by Prof. Huawei Huang. He received bachelor's degree in School of Computer Science and Engineering from Sun Yat-sen University in 2021. In the same year, he began his Master's studies in Software Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, guided by Prof. Neng Zhang, with a research focus on software engineering. Following this, he applied for a combined Master's and Ph.D. program and commenced his doctoral studies in 2023. His current research interests mainly include sharding blockchain technology.
